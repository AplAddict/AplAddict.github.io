About ICJAIC

The Ida Crown Jewish Academy Investment Club (ICJAIC) seeks to help students become financially educated.

The Club caters to people who would like to learn more about the stock market and investing in general. It manages the Ida Crown Jewish Academy Investment Fund, designed to help educate students on asset managment and will eventually benefit students financially through the funding of scholarships.

The Club holds a virtual investing competition on the investopedia stock market simulator, creates presentations, hosts guest speakers, and organizes stock pitches and debates to determine which companies belong in our portfolio.
